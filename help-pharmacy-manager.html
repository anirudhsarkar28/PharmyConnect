Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I manage inventory?

You can manage inventory by going to the Inventory Management section and adding, updating, or removing products from the system. You can also track stock levels and set reorder alerts.

2. How can I add new staff members?

To add new staff members, navigate to the User Management section and use the 'Add Staff' form. Enter the staff's details, such as name, email, and role, then click 'Add Staff'.

3. How do I view sales reports?

Sales reports can be accessed in the 'Reports and Analytics' section. You can view and download various sales data, including daily, weekly, and monthly sales summaries.

4. How do I contact customer support?

If you need further assistance, please fill out the support form below and submit your query. Our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
